# 🍔 Fast Food Nutritional Risk Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes the nutritional risk of fast food items using **Power BI** for visualization and **MySQL** for data processing and KPI generation.  
The goal is to identify high-risk food items based on calories, fat content, and a custom health score.

---

## 🔧 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI – Dashboard & Data Visualization  
- MySQL – Data Cleaning, Transformation & KPI Calculations

---

## 📊 Key KPIs
- Total Unique Items  
- Average Calories  
- Average Fat (g)  
- Highest Calorie Item  
- Category-wise Average Calories & Fat  
- Health Score (custom metric)  
- Top 5 High-Risk Food Items  
- Calorie Classification (Low / Moderate / High)  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- A large portion of food items fall into the **high-calorie category**  
- High fat content significantly impacts the **health risk score**  
- Certain categories dominate in both **calories and fat levels**  
- A small number of items contribute disproportionately to overall risk  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Introduce more **low-calorie and low-fat alternatives**  
- Highlight healthier options on menus  
- Reformulate high-risk food items  
- Improve nutritional awareness through labeling
